Introduction: Two Sign-in Methods & Setup Flow

😊
Face Recognition (Windows Hello)
Sign in automatically by looking at your PC camera. The easiest method.

Best for: No mask, well-lit, camera shutter open
🔢
PIN (Passcode)
Sign in by entering a number or alphanumeric code. Backup when face recognition is unavailable.

Best for: Wearing a mask, low light, or camera issues

💡 "I just entered my password — why do I need a PIN too?"
The password is used by Windows once for identity verification. The PIN is a short code used only on this device for subsequent sign-ins. It is never sent over the internet and stays on your PC, making it secure.
💡 About PIN
• Numbers only (4+ digits)
• Check "Include letters and symbols" to use alphanumeric characters
• Device-specific — cannot be used on other PCs
• Make sure to keep a note of your PIN

Common Issues & Solutions

😔 Face Not Recognized

"Could not recognize" message appears

✅ Solution

① Check that the privacy shutter is open
② Try without a mask
③ Check that the lighting is not too dim
④ Re-register via "Improve recognition"
⑤ If unresolved, sign in with PIN

🔢 Forgot PIN

Cannot remember PIN

✅ Solution

Click "I forgot my PIN" on the sign-in screen → Verify identity with Microsoft account or domain password → Reset your PIN. If unresolved, contact #help-it-support.

⚠️ "I forgot my PIN" Link Not Visible

The link does not appear on screen

✅ Solution

① First sign in with your password (sign-in screen → "Sign-in options" → "Password")
② After signing in: Settings → Accounts → Sign-in options → PIN → "Remove" → "Add" to reset
③ If your password is also unknown, contact #help-it-support

⚙️ "Set up" Button Not Available

"This option is currently unavailable" message appears

✅ Solution

This may be restricted by organization policy. Contact #help-it-support on Slack. The IT team will check the policy settings.

🔄 Want to Re-register Face Recognition

Recognition accuracy is poor and needs resetting

✅ Solution

Settings → Accounts → Sign-in options → "Face Recognition (Windows Hello)" → "Remove" → After removal, re-register via "Set up". Registering with and without glasses improves accuracy.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions about PIN Reset

Q. Will my data be deleted?
No. Resetting your PIN only changes the sign-in method and does not affect your files or apps.
Q. What if I also forgot my Microsoft password?
Contact #help-it-support on Slack. The IT team will handle the account reset.
Q. Do I need to re-register face recognition after resetting?
No. Face recognition settings are preserved. Changing your PIN does not affect face recognition.
Q. Can I change my PIN multiple times?
Yes, you can change it as many times as you like. Go to Settings → Accounts → Sign-in options → PIN (Windows Hello) → "Change" at any time.
