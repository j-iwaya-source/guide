Pre-Check: Before You Begin

📌 Please confirm all of the following conditions are met

✅ Required Permissions & Conditions
1
You are logged in with a Google Workspace Super Admin account.
2
Your plan is Google Workspace Business Starter or higher.
3
Employees have already set up passkeys on their devices (refer to the Windows Hello / Mac Passkey Setup Guide).
⚠️ [Important] Understand the scope of this change

Enabling this setting allows all users in the target OU to sign in by skipping the password.

Users who have not set up a passkey can still sign in with their password as usual.
⚠️ Note on third-party SSO (Keeper)

The Admin Console displays the following warning:
"This setting doesn't apply when users are authenticated by a third-party identity provider."

Since Keeper uses Google SSO, verify during the pilot test that passkey sign-in works end-to-end with Keeper.
💡 It is recommended to test with a specific OU first before rolling out company-wide.

📌 What changes — and what doesn't

✅ What becomes possible after enabling
· Sign in to Google without a password using a passkey
· Launch Keeper via SSO without a password (verify in pilot)
· Use a passkey instead of two-step verification
⚠️ What stays the same
· Users without a passkey can still sign in with their password
· Existing passwords are not deleted
· Admins can individually disable passkey sign-in

Common Issues and Solutions

🔍 "Passwordless" menu not found

The Passwordless option is not visible under Authentication

✅ Solution

Type "passwordless" in the Admin Console search bar to navigate directly. Also confirm your Google Workspace plan is Business Starter or higher.

🔒 Cannot check the checkbox

The checkbox is greyed out and cannot be clicked

✅ Solution

Check whether the currently logged-in account has Super Admin privileges. If not, ask a Super Admin to make the change.

⏱ No change after saving

Passkey sign-in still not working for employees

✅ Solution

Settings can take a few minutes (up to 24 hours) to propagate. Have employees clear their browser cache or try again in an Incognito window.

🔗 Passkey doesn't work with Keeper

Google passkey works but Keeper still asks for password

✅ Solution

This may be due to the third-party IdP limitation shown on the settings screen. Check Keeper's SSO configuration or contact the Corporate IT Team.

↩️ Want to revert the setting

Need to disable passkey password skip

✅ Solution

Go to the same screen, uncheck the checkbox, and click "SAVE". Users will then need to sign in with their password again.

❓ If none of the above resolves your issue, please reach out via the Slack #keeper-support channel or contact the Corporate IT Team directly.
