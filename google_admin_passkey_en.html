Pre-Check: Before You Begin

📌 Please confirm all of the following conditions are met

✅ Required Permissions & Conditions
1
You are logged in with a Google Workspace Super Admin account.
2
Your plan is Google Workspace Business Starter or higher.
3
Employees have already set up passkeys on their devices (refer to the Windows Hello / Mac Passkey Setup Guide).
⚠️ [Important] Understand the scope of this change

Enabling this setting allows all users in the target Organizational Unit (OU) to sign in by skipping the password.

Users who have not set up a passkey can still sign in with their password as usual.
💡 It is recommended to test with a specific OU first before rolling out company-wide.

📌 What changes — and what doesn't

✅ What becomes possible after enabling
· Sign in to Google without a password using a passkey
· Launch Keeper via SSO without a password
· Use a passkey instead of two-step verification
⚠️ What stays the same
· Users without a passkey can still sign in with their password
· Existing passwords are not deleted
· Admins can individually disable passkey sign-in

Passkey settings
Skip password when using passkey
On ← Click here to enable
When enabled, users with a passkey set up can skip password entry.
✅ Settings saved
1
From the left menu or home screen of the Admin Console, click "Security".
2
Under the Security menu, click "Authentication" → "Passkeys and security keys".
3
Click the toggle for "Skip password when using passkey" to turn it On.
4
If a confirmation dialog appears, click "Enable".
5
Click "Save" to confirm the setting.
✅ You are done when the toggle turns blue (On) and "Settings saved" is displayed.
⚠️ It may take up to 24 hours for the setting to take effect. If it doesn't appear to work during testing, please wait and try again.

Common Issues and Solutions

🔍 Setting not found

"Passkeys and security keys" menu is not visible

✅ Solution

Type "passkey" in the Admin Console search bar to navigate directly. Also confirm that your Google Workspace plan supports this feature (Business Starter or higher required).

🔒 Cannot change the setting

The toggle is greyed out and cannot be clicked

✅ Solution

Check whether the currently logged-in account has Super Admin privileges. If not, ask a Super Admin to make the change.

⏱ No change after saving

Passkey sign-in still not working for employees after enabling

✅ Solution

Settings can take up to 24 hours to propagate. Have employees clear their browser cache or try again in an Incognito window.

↩️ Want to revert the setting

Need to disable passkey password skip

✅ Solution

Go to the same screen, toggle the setting to "Off", and click "Save". Users will then need to sign in with their password again.

❓ If none of the above resolves your issue, please reach out via the Slack #keeper-support channel or contact the Corporate IT Team directly.
