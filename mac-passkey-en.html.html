Troubleshooting — Common Issues & Solutions

😕 Touch ID doesn't respond

Touch ID button is not recognizing your fingerprint

✅ Solution

Re-enroll your fingerprint: System Settings → Touch ID & Password → delete the fingerprint and add it again. Dry or dirty fingers are a common cause. You can also authenticate using your Mac password as a fallback.

☁️ "iCloud Keychain is not set up"

This message appears when trying to save to iCloud Keychain

✅ Solution

Go to System Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → turn on "Passwords & Keychain". An internet connection is required. After enabling, try creating the passkey again.

🚫 "Admin hasn't enabled passkey sign-in"

Organization policy prevents passkey use

✅ Solution

The IT team needs to enable "password skip" in the Google Admin Console. Please contact #keeper-support on Slack.

📱 Passkey not available on other Apple devices

iPhone or iPad not showing the synced passkey

✅ Solution

Confirm all devices are signed in with the same Apple ID. On the other device: Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → make sure "Passwords & Keychain" is turned on.

🔄 I want to delete and re-create my passkey

Need to reset or recreate a passkey

✅ Solution

Go to myaccount.google.com/signinoptions/passkeys → click the delete icon next to the passkey → then follow the same steps to create a new one.

💻 I lost or replaced my Mac

What happens to my passkey when changing devices?

✅ Solution

If saved to iCloud Keychain, your passkey will automatically sync to your new Mac when you sign in with the same Apple ID. Delete the old device's passkey from your Google account settings.

❓ If none of the above solves your issue, contact the Corporate IT Team via Slack: #keeper-support
