📌 Common Issues

Q. Keeper is not auto-filling
→ Click the Keeper icon and use the "→ Fill" button for the matching record.
Q. Chrome keeps showing "Save password?" popup
→ Confirm that Step 4 and Step 5 are complete.
Q. A device approval screen appeared after login
→ See the device approval instructions in Step 1. If no other device is available, contact Slack #keeper-support.
Q. Fewer passwords than expected after import
→ Check that you selected all passwords and clicked "Add to Keeper" during the import.
