Update Chrome Settings (Prevent Conflicts)

~3 min

[Important] This setting prevents Chrome from prompting "Save password?" after every login.

1
Click ⋮ (menu) → "Settings" in the top-right of Chrome.
2
From the left menu, click "Autofill and passwords" → "Google Password Manager".
3
Click "Settings" (gear icon) on the left side of the screen.
4
Turn "Offer to save passwords and passkeys" to Off (grey).
5
Restart Chrome after completing the setting.
